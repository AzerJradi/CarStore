Audi, a renowned German automobile manufacturer, is celebrated for its luxury vehicles, cutting-edge technology, and a rich automotive heritage. Founded in 1909, Audi has consistently delivered innovative engineering, performance, and design. The brand is known for its Quattro all-wheel-drive system and iconic models like the Audi A3, A4, A6, and A8 sedans, as well as the popular Q-series SUVs. Audi has also made significant strides in electric mobility with its e-Tron series. With a commitment to sustainability, advanced driver-assistance systems, and a strong presence in motorsports, Audi continues to be a leading player in the global automotive industry.
